UPCOMING FOUNDERS DAY CELEBRATION

Our beloved school was founder on the 12th of January, 1997.
Hence every year on the anniversary of our founders day,
the school celebrates by staging a cultural day celebration.
Our cultural day featuresa lot of activities including:
Debate, Dance presentations from different tribes, various games, bible recitation, Drama, and lots of other fun activities.
Also the school is serving indeginous food from different tribes in Nigeria including foods like:Afang
,Banga, Edikaikan, ifokore, etc.
Parents are welcome to join the celebration and are also allowed to bring their
cultural food and drinks for their wards.
please note that alcohol drinks and beverages are NOT ALLOWED
